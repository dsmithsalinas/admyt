# Admyt Brand Guidelines

## Name
**Admyt** — pronounced "admit." Coined spelling, AI-native feel.
Always written lowercase: `admyt`
The `y` is the brand accent — indigo (#818CF8) on dark, brand indigo (#6366F1) on light.

## Logo
Mortarboard icon — graduation cap viewed from above as a diamond.
The tassel doubles as a spark/pin-drop in Vibe Pink (#F0ABFC).

## Colors
| Name      | Hex     | Usage                        |
|-----------|---------|------------------------------|
| Midnight  | #0F172A | Primary background, nav      |
| Indigo    | #6366F1 | Primary brand, CTAs, icon bg |
| Lavender  | #818CF8 | Accent, wordmark `y`         |
| Vibe Pink | #F0ABFC | Vibe Check feature, accents  |
| Off-white | #F8FAFC | Page background              |

## Voice
Direct, warm, zero condescension. Feels like a knowledgeable older sibling — not a guidance counselor.

## Features
- **School Match** — AI college recommendations
- **Vibe Check** — Social and cultural fit analysis
- **Admit Odds** — AI-estimated admission likelihood

## Tagline
*find your fit. feel it first.*
